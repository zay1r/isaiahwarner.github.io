Car Dealership Dashboard

Data Collection

  • Data was collected a vehicle dataset form Kaggle.com
  • This data was stored as a CSV and exported into Jupyter notebook for data cleaning transformation
  • I cleaned this data using a python scrip that I created
  • I made filled out missing data in the Sold price, sold date, Sales commission, and Margin % columns
  • I made additional calculated and randomized columns and randomized column entries to fill in the missing data

Dashboard Design and Features

  • Created an interactive dashboard using Tableau
  • My car dealership Dashboard automatically updates the graphs when the users interact with the graphs
  • Included slicers for easy filtering of data by Year or Dealership
  • Used Graphs, Tables and CHarts to highlight trends and outliers in the data.
  • Descriptive Statistics: Mean, median, mode, and variance for shot patterns.
  • Comparative Insights: Sales to dat, Revenue, Profit Margin, Average sales ratting and average time on lot
  • Visual Representations: State Heatmap and bar charts and Tables for intuitive data exploration.

Results and Key Takeaways:

Your data analytics project showcases a well-structured approach to analyzing automobile sales, leveraging AI and Python to enhance data transformation and visualization. By cleaning and randomizing key financial fields, you ensured accuracy in revenue, profit margin, and commission calculations. The visualization provided valuable insights into dealership performance, showing December as the most profitable month and Maruti as the highest-selling car in terms of dollar value. Sales distribution remained balanced across energy types, while convertibles proved to have the longest turnover time. The most popular car colors—blue, black, and red—dominated sales, with North Carolina emerging as the most profitable state. The insights gathered can help dealerships optimize sales strategies, inventory management, and employee performance evaluation. Future improvements could include deeper predictive analytics, exploring factors influencing vehicle turnover, and fine-tuning location-based sales strategies for even greater business growth.
